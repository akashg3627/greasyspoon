Taste Buds is a eatery facility at IIT Indore managed by Bhopal Caterers. 
Bhopal Caterers is a professionally managed organization in catering and food business with over 12 years of experience. 
They are engaged in “On-site catering solutions” since 2001 and has achieved expertise in customized institutional catering. 
They are providing services at IIT Indore since April 2016.

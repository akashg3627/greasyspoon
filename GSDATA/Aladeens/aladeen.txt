Aladeens is a student run food venture founded by three students of 2014 batch of IIM Indore, Mr. Amogh Jawa, Mr. Jaihit Soni and Mr. Aashish Ranjan. Aladeens serves a delicious, healthy and rich North Indian cuisine. Whether it’s the Tikkas or Rotis, they believe in a little barbequing. 
The have a saying “If you can’t go to Delhi, we will bring Delhi to you.” 
They are providing services at IIT Indore since January 2018.
